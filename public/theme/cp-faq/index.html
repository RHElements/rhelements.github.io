    Test What are the benefits of becoming a Red Hat Amp member?

    Member Benefits:

    Becoming a Red Hat Amp member means being publicly recognized for their valuable contributions and achievements. As a part of this exclusive community group, Red Hat Amp members will have an opportunity to network with industry peers, gain valuable knowledge and insights, participate in product, service and technology discussions and represent the voice of the customer back into Red Hat.

    Red Hat Amp member benefits include (and are subject to change):

    • Recognition as a valued member of the Red Hat Amplifier program
    • Network with industry peers who share their same passion for Red Hat products, services and technologies
    • Expand their technical knowledge and gain valuable insights
    • Invitation to event guest speaking opportunities
    • Influence future product roadmaps
    • Receive cool branded gear
